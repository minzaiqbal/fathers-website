Who We Are

We are a professional tax consultancy and legal advisory practice based in Jamshedpur, Jharkhand. With years of hands-on experience in income tax, GST, and legal matters, we have helped hundreds of individuals, businesses, and organizations navigate the complexities of Indian taxation and law.

Our practice is built on three core values — accuracy, integrity, and client-first service. Every client's case is handled with personal attention and full confidentiality.
